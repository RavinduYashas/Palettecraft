About Palettecraft

Palettecraft is a free tool for designers and developers to find perfect color palettes and font combinations for their web projects. Our mission is to make good design accessible to everyone, regardless of their design experience.

We believe that beautiful websites shouldn't require expensive design software or years of experience. With Palettecraft, you can create professional-looking designs by simply selecting from curated options that work well together.

All our tools are completely free to use, with no sign-up required. We're committed to keeping Palettecraft accessible to the design community.
