About Palettecraft

Palettecraft is a free tool for designers and developers to find perfect color palettes and font combinations for their web projects. Our mission is to make good design accessible to everyone, regardless of their design experience.

We believe that beautiful websites shouldn't require expensive design software or years of experience. With Palettecraft, you can create professional-looking designs by simply selecting from curated options that work well together.

All our tools are completely free to use, with no sign-up required. We're committed to keeping Palettecraft accessible to the design community.

Privacy Policy

Palettecraft is committed to protecting your privacy. We don't collect personal information, and we don't use cookies to track you.

Data Collection

We don't collect any personal data. All color and font selections are processed locally in your browser. Images uploaded for color extraction are processed entirely on your device and never sent to our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts to load font families. This is subject to Google's privacy policy. No other third-party services are used.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of Palettecraft after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
